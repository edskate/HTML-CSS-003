In the simple tense of some verbs, we add es instead of (s) in the third person singular.

a) If a verb ends in (y) preceded by a consonant, we change the (y) to (i) and add es.

  • (B) If a verb ends in o, we add es.
  • (C) If a verb ends in s, sh, ch, x, or, z, we add es.
